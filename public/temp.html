About Deakin University

Deakin University, located in Victoria, Australia, is a prestigious institution known for its strong emphasis on innovative teaching, research, and engagement with industry and communities. Established in 1974, it has grown to become one of Australia's leading universities, with a strong focus on digital education and a commitment to providing a flexible learning environment.
